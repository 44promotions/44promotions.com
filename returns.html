Returns & Refunds

Our Policy: We want you to be completely satisfied with your purchase. If you're not happy with your order, we're here to help.

Returns Policy

  • Contact us within 7 days of delivery to request a return
  • Items must be unused and in original packaging
  • Custom or personalized items may not be eligible for return
  • Return shipping costs are the responsibility of the customer

Non-Returnable Items

  • Custom products with your logo or design
  • Products that have been used or altered
  • Items returned after 7 days of delivery
  • Products not purchased directly from us

Refunds

Once we receive and inspect your return, we'll notify you of the status of your refund. If approved:

  • Refunds will be processed within 5-7 business days
  • Refunds will be credited to your original payment method
  • Shipping charges are non-refundable

How to Request a Return

  1. Contact us at info@44promotions.com with your order number
  2. Describe the issue with your order
  3. Wait for our response with return instructions
  4. Ship the item back to us
  5. Receive your refund once inspected

Damaged or Defective Items

If you receive a damaged or defective item, please contact us immediately with photos of the damage. We'll arrange for a replacement or full refund at no cost to you.
