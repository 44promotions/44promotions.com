Printing Methods We Offer

Screen Printing

The most popular method for promotional products. Ink is pushed through a mesh stencil onto the product. Creates vibrant, long-lasting colors.

Best for: T-shirts, hoodies, tote bags

Embroidery

Thread is sewn directly into the fabric for a premium, textured look. Extremely durable and professional appearance.

Best for: Caps, polo shirts, jackets, bags

Heat Transfer

Design is printed on special paper and heat-pressed onto the product. Perfect for full-color designs and complex artwork.

Best for: Small orders, full-color logos

Laser Engraving

Laser burns the design into the surface for a permanent, premium finish. No ink or decals - it's part of the product itself.

Best for: Pens, drinkware, metal items

Digital Printing

Direct-to-product digital printing allows for photographic quality and unlimited colors. No setup fees or minimums.

Best for: Complex designs, small quantities

Sublimation

Ink is infused into the product surface for vibrant, full-coverage prints that won't fade or crack over time.

Best for: Mugs, mouse pads, phone cases

Frequently Asked Questions

What file formats do you accept?

We accept AI, EPS, PDF, SVG, PNG, and high-resolution JPG files. Vector formats (AI, EPS, PDF) are preferred for best print quality.

What is the minimum order quantity?

Minimum quantities vary by product and printing method. Screen printing typically requires 50+ items, while digital printing can be as low as 1 piece.

How long does production take?

Production time typically takes 7-14 business days after proof approval. Rush services are available for an additional fee.

Will I see a proof before production?

Yes! We provide a digital proof for your approval before any production begins. This ensures your logo looks exactly as expected.

Can I get color matching?

Yes, we can match PMS (Pantone) color codes for an accurate brand color match. Just provide your color codes when uploading your logo.
